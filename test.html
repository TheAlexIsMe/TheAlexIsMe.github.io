Official Group Rules

Welcome! As we grow toward becoming an official organization, we rely on all members to uphold a high standard of conduct and safety.

1. Respect All Campus Users

  • Prioritize Walkers: Always yield to pedestrians and give them plenty of space. Our goal is to promote a positive image for all two-wheeled riders on campus.
  • Be Courteous: Absolutely no harassment, discrimination, or offensive language toward any member or non-member.

2. Riding & Safety Standards

  • Safety First: Encourage and practice responsible riding. Know and follow all UH campus and local traffic rules.
  • No Reckless Behavior: Do not promote, organize, or encourage dangerous or illegal riding (e.g., riding too fast in crowded areas).

3. Group Focus

  • Scooters & Bikes: This group is primarily for scooter owners, but **bicycles are welcomed**. Keep discussions focused on transportation, tips, campus life, and events.
  • Stay Informed: Check the **Announcements** channel for official news and updates on our progress to become a recognized UH Student Organization.

Join the movement. Let's roll, Coogs!
